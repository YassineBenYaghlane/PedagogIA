**Tronc commun** = the FWB's polytechnic, pluridisciplinary common curriculum spanning **M1 → S3** (from age ~3 to end of 3rd year of secondary, ~14–15). Introduced by the [[entities/pacte-enseignement-excellence]] as a reinforcement and extension of what used to be a shorter, more narrowly academic common core.

Every student, whatever their later track, must master the tronc commun's *savoirs, savoir-faire et compétences* before differentiated filières open up in S4.

## Structure

Eight **domaines** cover the tronc commun:

- **Domaines spécifiques** (1–5): Français/arts/culture · Langues modernes · Maths/sciences/techniques · Sciences humaines / philo-citoyenneté / religion-morale · Éducation physique, bien-être et santé.
- **Domaines transversaux** (6–8): Créativité et esprit d'entreprendre · Apprendre à apprendre et poser des choix · Apprendre à s'orienter.

Each domaine is realized through one or more disciplinary référentiels (for maths, the one ingested as [[sources/referentiel-mathematiques]]).

## Relevance to PedagogIA

PedagogIA's POC addresses the math discipline within the tronc commun, **P1–P6 only** (Champ 3 arithmetic). Secondary (S1–S3) is future-scope — the wiki carries thin references for later expansion.
