The large-scale reform of French-speaking Belgian education launched by the FWB. Tagline: *"Le monde évolue. L'école aussi."* The [[sources/referentiel-mathematiques]] is one deliverable in this reform — part of a broader rewrite of curriculum, governance, and evaluation practices.

## Principles visible in the math référentiel

- **Double exigence d'équité et d'efficacité** — every student, regardless of background, should acquire a robust common base.
- **Tronc commun** M1→S3 (see [[concepts/tronc-commun]]) — extending the common curriculum to all students until 3e secondaire instead of splitting earlier.
- **Centration sur l'essentiel** — référentiels trimmed to what "*il n'est pas permis d'ignorer*", not everything it's possible to know.
- **Progressivité fluide** — annualized attendus to harmonize expectations across schools/networks.
- **Évaluation formative** as the primary orientation (see [[concepts/evaluation-formative]]).
- **Redoublement −50 % d'ici 2030** — through personalized support rather than year-repetition as first resort.
- **Modernisation** — digital tools, calculatrice from P3, explicit digital literacy ("littératie numérique").

## Governance artifacts

- Ten drafting groups (one per référentiel) composed of Inspection, FPO/PO pedagogical advisors, teachers, academics.
- A **Charte des référentiels** common to all groups, enforcing quality criteria (precision, coherence, realism).
- Supervision by the Commission des référentiels et des programmes.
- Iterative quantitative + qualitative review by field teachers before publication.

## Rollout timeline (math)

- 2022 — P1, P2
- 2023 — P3, P4
- 2024 — P5
- … yearly progression through **2028 — S3**
