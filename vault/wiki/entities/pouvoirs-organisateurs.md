The organizing authorities of schools in FWB. A PO is the legal entity responsible for a school or network of schools (e.g. Wallonie-Bruxelles Enseignement for official FWB schools, SeGEC for Catholic network, etc.). FPOs are federations that coordinate across POs.

## Role in the curriculum chain

Per the [[sources/referentiel-mathematiques]]:

- **FWB** publishes the *référentiels* (what to learn — decree-level).
- **POs / FPOs** author the ***programmes d'études*** from those référentiels ("les programmes d'études présentent les orientations méthodologiques, des dispositifs et des situations pédagogiques qui sont de nature à installer ces contenus").
- **Teachers** implement the programmes.

So a référentiel is *methodologically neutral* ("un cadastre méthodologiquement neutre"). It lists savoirs, savoir-faire, compétences and their attendus without prescribing how to teach them — that's the PO's programme. This gives POs genuine pedagogical freedom while keeping the learning targets common.

## Why this matters for PedagogIA

- Different schools (different POs) will teach the same attendus via *different methods*. PedagogIA must not lock into one particular methodological approach — our content should be compatible with whichever programme the student's school uses.
- PO-specific programmes (e.g. FédEFoC, WBE manuals) may be useful secondary sources for concrete activity ideas, but the référentiel is the authoritative target.
